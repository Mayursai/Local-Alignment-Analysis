BLAST online tool: https://blast.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/Blast.cgi
-> Click on Nucleotide Blast to compare DNA sequences. CLick on Protein Blast to compare Protein sequences.
-> Then either enter the sequence manually, or upload a .fasta file.
-> Then BLAST it and note down the results. 
-> Do this for all the test sequences.

FASTA online tool: https://www.ebi.ac.uk/jdispatcher/sss/fasta

Smith Waterman Online Tool: https://www.ebi.ac.uk/jdispatcher/psa/emboss_water?stype=dna&matrix=EDNAFULL
